how to come here
plane
Due do geological difficulties and no real conexion with anywhere else, the only feasible way to come here is by plane.

There's also the subject of not having direct flights from many places, so most probably will be a split fight to come here.

You will need a non-immigrant visa to enter or travel through Madagascar as a short term visitor for stays over 15 days, or an immigrant visa for stays over 90 days.
